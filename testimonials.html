Client Success Stories

Family Office Asset Rebalancing

We audited a Mumbai-based family office with existing assets of ₹12 Crores spread across multiple bank-distributed mutual funds. Our analysis identified high overlapping asset groups, heavy exposure to underperforming large-cap blocks, and high commission payouts.

Our rebalancing strategy consolidated their holdings into active target-maturity funds and direct equity advisory, reducing expense ratios by 1.2% per annum.

₹12 Cr Assets Managed
+14.2% CAGR Yield

Treasury Liquidity Restructuring

An agricultural tech firm had high working capital constraints during product seasons. They held significant mutual fund reserves but selling them triggered high short-term tax liabilities.

Phoenix architected a Loan Against Mutual Funds facility, enabling them to secure quick liquidity at highly competitive borrowing rates while keeping their core portfolios growing uninterrupted.

₹3.5 Cr Liquidity Secured
0% Tax Impact

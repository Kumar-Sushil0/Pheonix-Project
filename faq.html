Products & Minimums

What is the difference between Mutual Funds and Portfolio Management Services (PMS)?

Mutual Funds are pooled investment vehicles where small investments from thousands of investors are combined and managed collectively. PMS operates on individual client accounts under a direct advisory mandate, offering high customization and direct stock ownership, but requires a SEBI-regulated minimum ticket size of ₹50 Lakhs.

What is a Specialized Investment Fund (SIF)?

A Specialized Investment Fund (SIF) is a pooled portfolio structure designed to capture specific thematic growth sectors (such as renewable green infrastructure, digital technology, or specialized manufacturing opportunities) beyond traditional public index limits.

How do NIFTY Structured Products protect capital?

Structured products combine a fixed-income bond component (which matures to guarantee the initial principal capital at term) with a derivative component linked to NIFTY benchmarks. This architecture offers guaranteed capital safety alongside market-linked upside yield performance.

What is the minimum investment for Alternative Investment Funds (AIF)?

As per SEBI directives, the minimum investment requirement for an individual investor in a Category I or Category II AIF (covering private equity, venture capitals, or debt assets) is ₹1 Crore. Corporate or institutional mandates have higher base parameters.

Fees & Regulatory

Are your services fully regulated and compliant?

Yes, absolutely. Phoenix Financial Services strictly operates under regulatory compliance guidelines set by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) and the Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI). All transactions, fund structures, and reports are fully compliant.

How transparent are your fees and commission structures?

We believe in absolute clarity. All advisory fees, management fees for PMS, or commission sheets associated with mutual fund regular structures are disclosed in writing prior to deployment. We provide regular account statements showing all fee deductions.

How safe is my capital when investing through Phoenix?

All customer funds are routed directly into third-party depository accounts, AMCs, or clearing houses regulated by SEBI and RBI. Phoenix Financial serves strictly as your registered advisory panel; we do not store customer assets on our own balances.

Loans & Liquidity

Can I secure immediate cash liquidity against my active portfolio?

Yes. We offer Loan Against Shares (LAS) and Loan Against Mutual Funds (LAMF) solutions. By pledging your units as collateral, you can obtain flexible overdraft limits or short-term liquidity without liquidating your investments or triggering premature tax liabilities.

How is the interest calculated for Loan Against Shares?

Interest is calculated only on the actual amount drawn down and the duration for which it is used, not on the entire pledged value. The rates are highly competitive compared to RBI benchmarks.
